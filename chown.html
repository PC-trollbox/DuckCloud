Change owner of your VM
After you click "Change owner", DuckCloud will do the following:
1. Remove the access permissions from your DuckCloud ID
2. Give the DuckCloud ID specified below the access permissions.
NOTE 1: If you have launched the VM, the VM will still be launched and in the shell.
NOTE 2: If the user has no PRO flag and already has 1 machine, the ownership won't be changed and an error message will be shown.
